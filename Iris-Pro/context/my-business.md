# My Business

## What I'm Building
Iris — a SaaS accountability AIOS that communicates through Telegram. It helps people define their goals, set up a scheduled calendar, and then follows up to make sure they're actually doing the steps to reach their Mount Everest. When they're not, Iris helps refine the goal, the steps, all the pieces.

## Who It's For
Solopreneurs and small business owners who are trying to build and grow. People with goals, aspirations, and drive — not casual users. Specifically not for people who aren't business owners or aren't interested in starting a business.

## The Offer
- **Iris Core** (Free) — Lead magnet. A conversation for the Mount Everest experience. Lets people taste the accountability before committing.
- **Iris Pro** ($797) — Full download. Purchased through Lemon Squeezy. After payment, they get a download link immediately — zip file, easy setup.

## Where the Leads Come From
Warm network. Three years in the Pace Morby / Subto community. Bulk of leads will be warm connections — friends on Facebook, followers on Instagram.
